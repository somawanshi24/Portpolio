What is Photography?

Photography is the art and science of capturing light to create images. It's a way to freeze moments in time, tell stories, evoke emotions, and share perspectives. Photographers use cameras to capture these images, which can be anything from stunning landscapes to candid street scenes or carefully composed portraits. Photography involves a mix of technical skills (like understanding exposure, composition, and lighting) and creativity. Whether it's about capturing a breathtaking sunset, a smile, or even the mundane beauty of everyday life, photography allows us to see the world in a new and fascinating way.
